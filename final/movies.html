Amazing Spider-Man(2012-2014)
The film began development after the cancellation of Spider-Man 4 in 2010, with Sony Pictures Entertainment opting to reboot the franchise rather than continue Sam Raimi's series. It premiered on June 13, 2012 in Tokyo, and was released in US theaters on July 3, 2012. The film proved to be successful, both critically and financially. The first of at least three sequels was released in US theaters on May 2, 2014. However, Amazing Spider-Man are also great and wonderful for the fans.
